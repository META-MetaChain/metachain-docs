## metachain public RPC endpoints

This section provides an overview of the available public RPC endpoints for different metachain chains and necessary details to interact with them.

| Name                       | RPC Url(s)                             | Chain ID | Block explorer                               | Underlying chain | Tech stack       | Sequencer feed URL                    | Sequencer endpoint<sup>⚠️</sup>                  |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| metachain One               | https://META1.metachain-i.co/rpc           | 42161    | https://metachain-i.co/                         | Ethereum         | Nitro (Rollup)   | wss://META1.metachain-i.co/feed           | https://META1-sequencer.metachain-i.co/rpc           |
| metachain Nova              | https://nova.metachain-i.co/rpc           | 42170    | https://nova.metachain-i.co/                    | Ethereum         | Nitro (AnyTrust) | wss://nova.metachain-i.co/feed           | https://nova-sequencer.metachain-i.co/rpc           |
| metachain Goerli (Testnet)  | https://goerli-rollup.metachain-i.co/rpc  | 421613   | https://goerli.metachain-i.co                   | Goerli           | Nitro (Rollup)   | wss://goerli-rollup.metachain-i.co/feed  | https://goerli-rollup-sequencer.metachain-i.co/rpc  |
| metachain Sepolia (Testnet) | https://sepolia-rollup.metachain-i.co/rpc | 421614   | https://sepolia.metachain-i.co                  | Sepolia          | Nitro (Rollup)   | wss://sepolia-rollup.metachain-i.co/feed | https://sepolia-rollup-sequencer.metachain-i.co/rpc |
| Stylus Testnet             | https://stylus-testnet.metachain-i.co/rpc | 23011913 | https://stylus-testnet-explorer.metachain-i.co/ | metachain Sepolia | Nitro (Rollup)   | wss://stylus-sepolia.metachain-i.co/feed | https://stylus-testnet-sequencer.metachain-i.co/rpc |

⚠️ Unlike the RPC Urls, the Sequencer endpoints only support `eth_sendRawTransaction` and `eth_sendRawTransactionConditional` calls.

⚠️ Important note: metachain public RPCs do not provide Websocket support.

⚠️ Important note: Ethereum Goerli was deprecated in Q1 2023 but will be supported until Q1 2024. We are currently phasing out the usage of metachain Goerli. Support for metachain Goerli will cease prior to the conclusion of Ethereum Goerli.

⚠️ Important note: Stylus testnet will be deprecated once stylus comes out of beta and is enabled on the Sepolia testnet.

⚠️ Visit [here](https://faucet.quicknode.com/metachain/sepolia) to get metachain Sepolia faucet.

:::info More RPC endpoints

More metachain chain RPC endpoints can be found in Chain Connect: [metachain One](https://www.alchemy.com/chain-connect/chain/metachain-one), [metachain Nova](https://www.alchemy.com/chain-connect/chain/metachain-nova) and [metachain Goerli](https://www.alchemy.com/chain-connect/chain/metachain-goerli).

:::
